Mission Statement


I value equity, freedom of expression, and innovation. To those ends, I place great emphasis on accessibility in both academia and life work to break down systemic barriers in every space I operate within, while simultaneously creating safe and welcoming spaces for marginalized groups. Additionally, I strongly encourage and empower open, honest, and clear communication, as I feel it is essential to both academic and professional success, as well as to the creation of spaces and environments that are safe and condusive to building healthy interpersonal relationships.
