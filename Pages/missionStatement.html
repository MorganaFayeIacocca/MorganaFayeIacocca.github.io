Mission Statement


Among my core beliefs, I value equity, freedom of expression, and innovation. To those ends, I actively work to break down systemic barriers in every space I operate within, while simultaneously creating safe and welcoming spaces for marginalized groups. Additionally, I strongly encourage and empower open, honest, and clear communication, as I feel it is essential to both academic and professional success, as well as to the creation of spaces and environments that are safe and condusive to building healthy interpersonal relationships.



Experiences in Leadership and Community


Pagans at Pitt


For the past year, I have served as the Business Manager for the Pagans at Pitt student club, an organization built to create a space where all students who practice witchcraft, paganism, and similar practices. As a board member, I have taken part in making decisions for the club, and I have also helped plan and organize supplies for club events and celebrations.


League of Legends at Pitt


I continue to serve as the Mental Health Liaison for the League of Legends at Pitt club, which serves as a space for people to engage and connect over a shared interest in League of Legends and other similar games. In addition to creating a space to network, the club organizes an internal tournament every semester that empowers friendly competition and encourages teamwork.


Ultra-ViOLETS


I continue to be an active member of the Ultra-ViOLETS (Ultra VIsibile Out LGBTQ+ Experimentalists, Theorists, and Staff), club, an organization meant to create a safe and united space among Pitt physics students who are queer or who consider themselves to be allies and wish to learn from queer experiences.
